KONRAD ZUSE

Konrad Zuse was born on 22 June 1910 , in Berlin. He is civil engineer , inventor and entrepreneur . In his youth years and in his education life , he descriped himself as ‘ rover student ’ because he permanently got education in different cities because of his father’s job . He studied lycee in Hosianum Lycee during 9 years . In 1923 , his father was assigned to Hoyerswerda , so he continued to Reform Lycee . He passed from ripeness exam in 1928 . He got his higher education in Berlin-Charlottenburg Technical High School . In addition , he got architecture education and studied structural engineering . He finished his engineering education with certificate in 1935 . Firstly , he began job in Henschel Plane Factories as statiker but after a few short time he leaved from this job and he installed studio in his house of family . He designed that Z1 is the first mechanic calculator which can be program . After that , he developed Z1 and he designed that Z2 is the first numerical and having a memory computer . He installed ‘Zuse Engineering and Projection Berlin ’ . This company is single company about making calculator. In conclusion , he installed the first computer company in Germany with Z4 after second world war . In his retirement , he was interested with art and he writed a book ‘Kuno See’.
